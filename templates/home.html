About Us

The innovative website uses advanced algorithms and a thorough analysis of health data to accurately predict who might be at risk of heart diseases. This tool not only spots potential health issues but also gives users clear advice on what to do next, greatly improving preventive care and making it easier for everyone to stay healthy.
